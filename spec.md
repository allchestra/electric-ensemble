# Spec

## Browser Score

- The Electric app renders a single treble stave in G major.
- The browser draws the stave with VexFlow.
- The score alternates every four seconds between:
  - D, F-sharp, and A as a semibreve chord.
  - C, E, and G as a semibreve chord.
- The chord selection is owned by the server and propagated to the browser through Electric.
